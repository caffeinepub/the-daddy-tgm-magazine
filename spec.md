# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Blend early 2000s athletic/sports visual accents into the existing 1970s retro magazine design without replacing the core theme.

**Planned changes:**
- Apply a bold condensed sans-serif athletic font (Impact-style) to issue numbers, page indicators, and select section headers throughout the app
- Style issue numbers on the Home page and page indicators in the Reader with large jersey-style numeral treatment
- Add at least two decorative CSS diagonal stripe or swoosh dividers between layout sections (e.g., below masthead, between content sections)
- Introduce sporty color accents — crisp white and deep navy/black — as highlights alongside the existing rust, mustard, and avocado palette
- Update navigation hover states with an athletic-style treatment (swoosh underline, bold transform, or stripe flash)
- Keep the 1970s newsprint texture, retro serif masthead, earth tones, and monospace body text as the dominant visual base

**User-visible outcome:** The app reads as a 1970s retro magazine with added sports-mag energy — featuring athletic numerals, diagonal stripe accents, sporty nav hovers, and navy/white contrast highlights layered over the unchanged newsprint foundation.
